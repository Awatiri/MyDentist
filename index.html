About Madison Kids Dental

Welcome to Madison Kids Dental, where creating positive dental experiences for children is our passion! Founded with the belief that every child deserves gentle, compassionate dental care, we've been serving the Madison, Tennessee community for over 15 years.

Dr. Sarah Johnson, our board-certified pediatric dentist, completed specialized training in children's dentistry and child psychology. She understands that each child is unique and tailors her approach to meet individual needs, ensuring comfortable visits that build confidence and trust.

Our state-of-the-art facility is designed with children in mind - from our colorful, interactive waiting area to our gentle treatment rooms equipped with the latest pediatric dental technology. We believe that a positive first impression sets the stage for a lifetime of good oral health.
